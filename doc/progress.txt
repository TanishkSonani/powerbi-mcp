  src/discovery/port_finder.py — three-tier port discovery:
  - find_desktop_port_files() reads msmdsrv.port.txt from the AnalysisServicesWorkspaces folder
  - find_desktop_ports_via_netstat() cross-references tasklist + netstat as fallback
  - probe_xmla_instance(port) POSTs a minimal XMLA Discover SOAP envelope and parses the model name from the response
  - find_desktop_instances() combines all three, deduplicates, and returns [{model_name, port, connection_string}]
